About me

Hello, I am Binh Duc Mai. I am pursuing a B.Eng. at PTIT (2021 – 2026) with a current CPA of 3.83, under the supervision of Dr. Cong Tran. Currently, I am working as a Research Assistant at Faculty of AI - PTIT, where I focus on generative models for audio. My research interests include generative models, multimodal models, computer vision, natural language processing, and audio/signal processing.

I look forward to the opportunity to pursue a PhD after graduation.

LISA Chatbot

Link

Lisa is a multilingual chatbot developed for international students studying at PTIT. It supports three languages — Vietnamese, English, and Lao — to assist students in accessing academic information, navigating campus life, and connecting with university resources more easily.

Chatbot RAG

MBA Chatbot

Link

The MBA Chatbot is a comprehensive system designed to support MBA students throughout their studies. It consists of 12 specialized chatbots, each corresponding to a specific course, along with one general chatbot that provides overall academic assistance. Together, they help students with subject-specific guidance, course-related information, and general support for their MBA program.
